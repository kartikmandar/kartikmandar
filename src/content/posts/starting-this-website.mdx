After hours of retries I decided to try and make a fresh vercel project and add content to the CMS manually as I go. 



I guess I can better curate things as I go now. 



Nevertheless this post is one of the first. There will be many more but I don't know when and how these would come. I plan to make detailed posts about what happens in the life of academia people. 

It serves two purposes, either motivated school students can get inspired from it and join the scientific community  or people who thought this to be a shiny medal to achieve will drop that goal and search for something more practical. 



Academic life is no doubt really rewarding in some aspects but for sure is not the easiest path to money. There are much more pracical avenues in that respect.
