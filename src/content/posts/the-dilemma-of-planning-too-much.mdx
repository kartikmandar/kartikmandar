There are some people who can YOLO their way across anything, while there are others who think too much before doing anything. Analysing and planning everything before doing it. I have been guilty of the latter far too many times. One lesson that I learnt from all this is to sometimes just do things even when you are not ready. There is never a perfect time. Just give your shot.
